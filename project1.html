Recipe Ad Design

Working with retailers, media agencies, and sales teams to develop innovative new ad units that combine branded recipes and AdAdapted’s core technology.

Overview

Problem

AdAdapted's new Payload technology required a fresh approach to ad creatives. I was tasked with designing a variety of new ad units in highly engaging ways, moving beyond traditional banner adds to drive consumer action.

Solution

Designing a series of interactive and visually compelling ad units, including banners and interstitial pages, that seamlessly integrate recipes and CPG product media.

Users

Internal sales and marketing teams; CPG brands and media agencies.

Impact

Combining recipes with AdAdapted’s Payload technology gives CPG brands and large retailer clients a more customizable and innovative way to add products to customers’ carts, opening new opportunities within the CPG and grocery ad tech landscape.

The new ad units boosted brand awareness and cart conversion by attracting and engaging both new and loyal customers through a unique and interactive visual experience.

Ad Design

Our designs focused on creating ad units that would not only appeal to consumers but also provide powerful tools for our clients to meet their marketing goals. Our strategy was to leverage our innovative Payload technology to creatively inspire consumers to act on adding products to their list or cart directly from an ad unit. The following showcases new ad formats and their key features:

1. Interactive Recipes

These ad units allow consumers to browse a recipe and select specific products to add directly to their retailer’s cart. It gives users the flexibility to choose exactly what they need without leaving their current recipe experience. The products will appear in their cart the next time they visit their retailer’s site.

In additon to ad unit renderings, I designed and engineered a user-friendly web application to enable our retailer and agency clients to easily customize the appearance of a cart confirmation modal. I leveraged front-end libraries, AI-driven code generation, and Figma's color validation to streamline its configuration alongside our SDK. Preview the app (without SDK use) here: Modal Configurator App

2. Recipe Interstitials

These ad units give consumers the flexibility to complete a variety of tasks - adding the product directly to their cart or list, sending it to their phone, or viewing a recipe. When a user selects "view recipe" from a banner ad, they are taken to a detailed recipe page. This visual experience includes AdAdapted's core technology, allowing them to add products directly to their cart or list while also engaging them in scalale recipe content.

I designed and developed a recipe interstitial page myself which takes URL parameters to display product and recipe information. Preview the interstitial page here: Recipe Interstitial

3. Recipe-Specific Banner Ads

These scrollable banner ads can display multiple products within a small space. Allowing the viewer to scroll within an ad unit increases engagement and allows us to advertise a recipe's full list of ingredients (multiple products!) in a single unit.
