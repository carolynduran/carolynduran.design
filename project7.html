Photography Work

I like to exercise my creative brain with photography!

Check out some of the photos I’ve taken throughout my recent travels.

Northern California

This past summer, I traveled to San Francisco, Napa Valley, and Yosemite National Park in California. What a trip! These are some of the most beautiful views I’ve ever seen.

Colorado & Montréal

Skiing is my favorite winter activity. These photos are from the snowy Rocky Mountains of Colorado and Mont Tremblant in Québec, Canada.

Up North, Michigan

Michigan is a wonder in the summer and fall. Since living here, I’ve enjoyed exploring its gorgeous lakeshores, quaint towns, cider mills, and historical landmarks. My favorite places to visit have been Mackinac Island, Sleeping Bear Dunes, and Old Mission Peninsula.

New Mexico

I’ve loved traveling to New Mexico to visit family, enjoy its peaceful landscapes, and explore its delicious food. I’ve also attended the Albuquerque International Balloon Fiesta, the largest balloon festival in the world.
